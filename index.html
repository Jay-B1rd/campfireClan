Robur's Vessel

After the clan suffered a terrible accident resulting in massive casualties, Robur became the leader of a handful of traumatized Nomai, all of them struggling in the aftermath of the disaster. Now, the clan is flourishing, well-known among the Nomai for taking in strays and those who don't fit in. They're proud of their progress, and are always looking for new faces.
