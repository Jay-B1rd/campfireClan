Robur's Vessel

After the clan suffered a terrible accident resulting in massive casualties, Robur became the leader of a handful of traumatized Nomai, all of them struggling in the aftermath of the disaster. Now, the clan is flourishing, well-known among the Nomai for taking in strays and those who don't fit in. They're proud of their progress, and are always looking for new faces.

Welcome to the Clan of Kindling.

Aerva

Your local delusional storyteller, with all too much whimsy and magic in her, lost in her own daydreams and imagination. Accompanied by the presence of an old friend, someone called "The Star Wanderer" that she can only sense.


Alaria

Alaria is an older teenager who's spent her entire life traveling from clan to clan. Her parents told her she was cursed, constantly having to wander or risk or life, but here in Robur's clan she feels she might have finally found a permanent home.


Allia (Browallia)

Amaryllis

Amaryllis is a young Nomai who was raised communally by her clan after the accident that befell the clan. An orphan from a very young age, her survival was nothing short of a miracle, being only an infant when the disaster happened. She views everyone as family, even if they’re new to the clan, they are family no matter what.

Hedera

One of the survivors of the accident, Hedera is a gruff machinist who still bears the scars of the fire both on his body and in his mind. Mostly known in the community for his sharp wit and no-nonsense demeanor, he can be surprisingly kind. He doesn't like remembering the accident, but he'll work as hard as he must to in order to ensure nothing like that ever happens again.

Typha

Typha is a young meteorologist who survived the accident and grew up in Robur's clan. Despite their usual assignment to monitor space weather aboard the vessel, their passions lie in planetary meteorology - on-the-ground research of unique weather patterns is what Typha dreams of focusing on one day.
