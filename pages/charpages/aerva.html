Appearance
A Nomai on the shorter side with a curly light brown mane. Her horns are the typical horns, going behind her ears. She mostly wears clothes inspired by Ancient Greek aesthetics, including long plain dresses accessorized with jewelry.
Personality Skills
Outgoing and energetic despite what had happened to her in the past. Happy to engage in conversations and of course tell stories. Deeply empathetic and emotional- although she doesn't show that clearly. Often confuses reality with her imagination and dreams, and snapping out of it can be quite hard. Happy to help where she can and just... be around! She's all too excited really, but deep down she craves solitude and something calmer. Life to her is a performance.
- Play the harp, lyre, flute, along with other instruments - Singing and acting - Basic survival skills including: Sewing Cooking Taking care of small injuries

I didn’t know back then.

She had admitted quietly. No one was exactly meant to hear it, but here they were, standing opposite of eachother.

Her hair undone and messy, hands nervously fidgeting with the edge of her earring, and eyes tired after days of no real sleep.

She was scared. Years and years spent denying what had happened all amounted to this it seemed.

You took my hand a- and walked me out.

Her memories were fuzzy, they always were. As time progressed they’d only get more and more muffled, mixed with fiction.

An ‘’emergency’’ they were yelling. We were both injured- Others were injured.

The sound of heavy things that shouldn’t break- breaking. Distant talking. Footsteps all around, quick, quick, quick, evacuate, they’ll be here soon, get out, be safe.

There were two other clans there to help us. They- Left me behind.

Silence.

You took my hand and told me to sing.

Sweet, sweet silence.

You promised to come find me.

…


And there, standing in the middle of the Observatory long after lights had gone out. Almost as if preparing for a ritual, she breathed-

You saved me at the cost of your life, didn’t you?

Somewhere, sometime, a new star was born.
