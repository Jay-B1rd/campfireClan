Appearance
Typha is a piebald Nomai with russet brown and off-white fur. Their eyes are a light blue, their horns pale. They often accessorise with lots of jewellery, including several bronze antler rings, stud earrings, and a septum ring. Their fur is wavy and easily frizzes up, so they rigorously braid their mane to keep loose fur out of their way. They wear a tight-fit top beneath a loose-fitting wrap and comfortable pants. They hate dressing up for big occasions, and instead opt to wear simple clothes with more elaborate accessories so they don't feel constricted or uncomfortable.
Personality Skills
Typha is outgoing and eager to make friends, playful and dry-witted. While passionate, they don't usually display strong emotions unless they are around people they trust. Typha has fears of abandonment that motivate them to form a strong network with those around them, and they often try to take on extra work to make themself indispensable. When upset, they become quiet and retreat into themself.
- Quick-witted and good at improvisation - Athletic - Able to communicate across disciplines - Excellent at braiding fur
